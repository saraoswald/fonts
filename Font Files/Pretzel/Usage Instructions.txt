Usage Notes:
    - To figure out how install this font, please Google it :^)
    - Hearts are mapped to their unicode equivalent, and can be accessed in InDesign and Illustrator from the Glyphs Panel. 
     - Contextual Alternates: 
	- Crossbar-I 
	- < + 3 = ♥
	- . + . + . = …
	- ~ + ~ = 〜
	- ~ + ~ + ~ = 〰
     - Here's a list of where all the special characters are mapped:
            - Open Heart: ♡
            - Filled Heart: ♥
            - Open Star: ☆
            - Filled Star: ★
            - Long Wavy Dash: 〜
            - Longer Wavy Dash: 〰
